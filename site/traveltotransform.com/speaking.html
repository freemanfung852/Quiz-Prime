Why You Want Freeman for Your Next Event?

Freeman believes that when you embark on a journey of self-discovery to unlock your true potential, you not only elevate your own life but shape a better world collectively. His mission is to awaken more world citizens and change the world through accelerating personal transformations.

Over the last decade, Freeman has spoken on global stages at TEDx, Mindvalley & UNWTO, collaborated on top 5% globally ranked podcasts, as well as coached world-record-breaking athlete and impact-driven executives & entrepreneurs who aspired to improve humanity.

Besides touching thousands through his bestselling books and Travel To Transform programmes & workshops, Freeman is best known for guiding individuals to become thriving global citizens. His unique approach integrates personal development, world exploration, and conscious living—empowering individuals to lead purposeful lives and contribute positively to the global community.

When not on a plane, you'll find Freeman also pursuing his multi-passionate adventures as a content creator, DJ/musician, Shaolin martial artist, biohacker, and lucid dreamer!
