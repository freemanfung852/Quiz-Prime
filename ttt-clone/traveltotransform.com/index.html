About Freeman Fung

Freeman is an inspirational speaker, bestselling author, and certified life coach who has advocated New Global Citizenship at TEDx, Mindvalley, world tourism forums, and more. Through his movement, Travel To Transform, he empowers individuals to unlock profound freedom by transforming both their inner and outer journeys.
Originally from Hong Kong, he left home solo at age 19 to live in Romania for a Global Village project. What began as an escape from his struggling, mundane life evolved into a transformative journey across more than 35 countries over a decade, shaping him into a thriving global citizen.
Today, Freeman has touched the lives of thousands through his bestselling books and programmes, coaching world-record-breaking athletes and impact-driven entrepreneurs who aspire to improve humanity. His approach is uniquely recognised for integrating personal development, world exploration, and conscious living, empowering individuals to lead purposeful lives and positively contribute to the global community.
A true believer in the power of the mind, with a heart inspired by cultural diversity and inclusion, he is on a mission to awaken more world citizens and make our world a better place through personal transformations.
