FAQ's

1) I don't get to travel often at the moment. Will this course still be beneficial for me?

Absolutely! If you’re feeling that creating a global life sounds too far-fetched from your current reality… I’ve been there too! The key to travel mastery isn't about how many countries you get to visit, but your awareness and relationship with travel.

This course provides you with the tools, mindset, and action plan to transform your travel experiences and equip you for a global lifestyle, regardless of how often you travel.

It's worth mentioning that many students find they travel more frequently after completing this course because they take actual steps to implement the blueprint they create, which works with their current financial or personal responsibilities. Everyone is capable of mastering travel in the modern world today. Even though it's seldom taught in traditional education, it's a learnable skill!

2) Why a Blueprint? Why is this different from other traditional personal growth courses?

You might even wonder, "Do I really need this course to learn about travelling?" or "Why can't I just follow what travel influencers do?" The problem is—no one knows your dreams like you do!

Travelling without clear intentions is like flying a plane without a GPS. Sure, the view might be great, but does that truly take you to where you want to be?

That's why the key is to build your journeys from the inside-out, ensuring you reach your personal travel goals authentically! Unlike traditional cookie-cutter self-development courses, this TMB quest is tailored to help you discover your own greatness & strengths and achieve your unique travel aspirations.

Investing in yourself gives you more time and clarity, so you can spend more time actually enjoying your travels and building meaningful connections while spending less time on trial & error!

3) How is this course delivered?

The course is delivered entirely online, giving you the flexibility to start anytime and access the materials from anywhere in the world. You can pause, speed up, or rewatch the training to fit your schedule.

While designed as a 21-day program with daily exercises in bite-size formats (total 9+ hours of on-demand training), feel free to go at your own pace. You also own this programme for a lifetime, allowing you to rely on the resource-based lessons during your travels and update your blueprint anytime you're ready to unleash your next level of travel mastery.

If you need support, feel free to email [email protected].

4) Does it mean I have to live a full-time nomad lifestyle?

Absolutely not! TMB is about authentic travel and creating a travel blueprint that works for YOU holistically. You could be working full-time or have other commitments. Ultimately, the course integrates travel as a meaningful part of your life, regardless of your lifestyle.

Even in my own decade of travel, I wasn't living a nomadic lifestyle most of the time. Different life stages bring different opportunities, but clarity on travel mastery guides me.

That said, if after going through the course you feel inspired to pursue a nomadic lifestyle because that aligns with your inner values, rest assured we have resources to guide you in making that dream a reality too. This includes expert bonus interview, such as with Markos Korvesis, Co-Founder of Nomad365 and Head Career Coach of LeadCompass, where you'll learn the strategies, options, and actionable steps to take your next step into digital nomadism.

5) I'm not an experienced traveler. Can beginners take this course?

Definitely! This course is designed for all levels. Whether you're a seasoned traveller or just starting, you'll learn to cultivate your authentic relationship with travel, equip with practical tools to help accelerate your success, and navigate the modern world with confidence.

If you're starting out (congrats!), this course will bring new awareness and clarity, so you know how to use travel to elevate your personal & professional life. If you're already a frequent traveller or living a nomadic lifestyle, this course will take you to the next level of purposeful travel, aligning your mind, body, and spirit, and enhancing your global impact.
